The arXiv tagline, "arXiv Connects...", reflects our role as a place of connection among researchers, institutions, repositories, other Xivs, and to the world of open science. It re-enforces the message behind arXivs logo and adds specific context through word and image choice.

> “arXiv is often the first place where researchers take action in open science.”
> —Dr. Hideaki Takeda, Professor, National Institute of Informatics

Our tagline is both consistent and flexible. It always begins with "arXiv Connects" in a semi-bold weight, with the second line starting in lowercase and in a regular weight. No punctuation is ever used.

The font is Freight Sans Pro (in Google docs, use Catamaran. When other fonts are not available default to Arial). The higlight color is always black with the text in Light Warm Grey.

![Tagline example 1](images/image_name.jpg){.mkd-img-left}

By changing the context of the second line arXiv can draw attention to the myriad ways our service connects the world's researchers, and beyond.

![Tagline example 2, no image](images/image_name.jpg){.mkd-img-left}
![Tagline example 3, no image](images/image_name.jpg){.mkd-img-left}

The tagline is most often, though not exclusively, overlayed on top of a photographic image that can add further context, build a sense of place, or humanize our purely digital product.

![Tagline example 4 over image](images/image_name.jpg){.mkd-img-left .mkd-img-border}
![Tagline example 5 over image](images/image_name.jpg){.mkd-img-left .mkd-img-border}

 Images usually bleed off the edge on atleast two sides. Content should be aligned with the edge of the image while the tagline 'breaks' out of the grid.
